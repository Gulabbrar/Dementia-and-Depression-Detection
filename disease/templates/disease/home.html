A depression detection form is a tool designed to assess an individual's level of depression based on a set of questions or criteria. It aims to identify symptoms of depression and provide an indication of the severity or presence of the condition. The primary purpose of a depression detection form is to screen individuals for possible depression. It serves as an initial step in identifying those who may require further evaluation or support.The form typically consists of a series of questions related to various aspects of mental health, emotions, behaviors, and physical symptoms. The questions are carefully designed to assess common signs and symptoms of depression.
